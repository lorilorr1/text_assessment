Schuetz 1 Chloe Schuetz James Josue ENC 1102 February 26, 2017 The Good, the Bad, the Meat When it comes to cutting meat out of diet many people do it for health benefits. Others do it for the sake of protecting animals and their rights. Vegetarianism and Veganism are spreading like wildfire all around the world. This is causing more anti-meat advertisements. In these advertisements they use rhetorical elements to help get the point across to the audience. These elements include kairos, ethos, pathos, and logos.
